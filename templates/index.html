    • What happened?

    Anitai was originally meant to be a modular web-based way to view Manga and images. Anitai started development around 4/16. Over 9,000 lines of code were put into Anitai's original model. Unfortunately, a partner decided they would volunteer for work they had absolutely 0 intention of doing, thus, leaving me between a rock and a hard-place. I was forced to decide between running Anitai on my own, or scratching the project entirely. I currently work on average 50 hours a week, which does not leave me time to run a full-fledged community. But I also didn't want to scratch the idea of an anime-based site to deliver content.
